ARGUMENT

the act of having an abortion is murder due to life being considered to start at conception, and the legalization of abortion will create a culture where life is not as important.

POINT 1

life begins at conception (when sperm cell fertilizes egg cell, beginning process of cell division), hence abortion being murder
  • the moment sperm fertilized the egg, a new, distinct organism is created due to it having its own DNA it is different from their parents and other humans
  • and thus, it is considered that this life, while being simply a few cells, is still a life and ending it would be murder just like grown humans.

POINT 2

promotes culture where life becomes disposable
  • abortion failing to recognize the sanctity of human life by ending it targets poor women as needing a quick solution to a complicated issue.
  • abortion stems from the same root of selfishness, reflecting a sentiment that people are not equal
      • abortion is rife with potential for eugenics
    • ideology of fetuses being less than human combined with legalized abortion can lead to the normalization of creating babies with cherry picked features/qualities.

POINT 3

the act of abortion can be deemed unnecessary with increased access to birth control, health insurance, and sexual education
  • abortion rate decline can be attributed to improved safety + availability of LARC (long-acting reversible contraception)
    • abortion rates have declined since its peak in 1981
      • 1981: 29.3 abortions per 1000 women between ages 15-44
      • 2019: 11.4 abortions per 1000 women
  • teens are having sex later in life than their parents, due to improved sexual education
    • now, 38.4% of American high schoolers reported having sex and 27.4% reported being sexually active
      • back in 1991, 54% of American high schoolers reported having sex and 37.5% were sexually active.
